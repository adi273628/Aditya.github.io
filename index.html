Intro

Hey,
I’m Aditya Singh, presently enrolled in PGDM – Finance 21-23 batch at Arun Jaitely National Institute of Financial Management (AJNIFM), Faridabad, Haryana.

I’m being trained highly & professionally in the concepts of Financial Markets. As a Finance Student. I started my journey with the basics like Financial Accounting, Functionalities of Financial Institutions, and Legal Aspects Of Business. In the middle of my financial journey subjects like Cost & Financial Management, Quantitative Methods, SORM, Equity Research, Bank management, Fixed Income and Financial Econometrics have come across.

With my ongoing journey, I became passionate towards solving Cases, especially for those companies which are having problems in the finance area. To pursue my passion along with my studies, I joined various consulting clubs & societies and started to participate in case study competitions

At present, I have participated in about 40+ Case study competitions across colleges in India. In the coming future, I have plans to explore World Case Study Competitions. As you solve cases you become prominent in Pitching Business ideas in various domains. Business ideas are needed in various ways by businesses to grow, compete and stay alive in today’s era.

As along, you can see my certificates regarding the same, with a promise to grow more.

Once I heard a quote saying: “Until you cross the bridge of your insecurities, you can’t begin to explore your possibilities.” By: -Tim Fargo  in due course changed my direction of sailing.
